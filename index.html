Ph.D. Student in Civil and Environmental Engineering, UCLA.

Hi Everyone!
I am a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineeering at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Enrique López Droguett. Previously I obtained my B.E. and M.S in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Chile.

My research interests are Deep Learning, Quantum Computing, Probabilistic Graphical Models and how those topics can be applied to practical engineering applications to improve the safety and sustainability of large, interconnected systems. In particular, I am currently researching sampling and inference techniques based on Quantum Computing to aide the design and risk assessment of wildfire evacuation strategies.
