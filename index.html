Gabriel San Martín's Webpage (Me)

He/him. Welcome to my website. Here you can find links to my recent publications.

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Civil Engineering at UCLA. These are my research interests:

  • Quantum Computing, both its capabilities and limitations
  • Probabilistic risk assessment of critical infrastructure
  • Wildfires in general
